Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about living at Zentro

1 What is included in the rent?

All rooms come with Premium Bed and Mattress, food (breakfast, lunch, dinner), Wi-Fi, regular housekeeping, Washing Machines, and Chilled/Hot water dispensers.

2 Is food provided for Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner?

Yes, we provide nutritious South & North Indian meals including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Weekly 2-3 times Non-veg

3 What are the security arrangements?

We have 24/7 CCTV monitoring and dedicated security staff to ensure your safety and peace of mind.

4 Are there any restrictions on timings?

We maintain flexible entry timings for working professionals while ensuring a peaceful environment for all residents. Late entry is accommodated with prior notice.

5 What is the minimum stay period?

We offer flexible stay options from monthly to yearly packages. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements and get customized pricing.

6 Is parking available?

Yes, we provide secure parking facilities for two-wheelers only to all our residents at no additional cost.

7 Is Alcohol or Smoking allowed in the building?

Strictly NO. No alcohol or smoking allowed in the building.

8 How do you charge electricity bill?

Prepaid electricity meters installed for every room. From the smart phone app you can recharge as per your usage and see your balance

9 Is there a Launtry Room Service?

Yes, we offer Laundry Room Service on request. A service person will collect your used clothes from the laundry bag in your room. They will be washed, ironed, and neatly placed on your bed by the next day for a small additional cost

10 Do I get vegitarian food for all days?

Yes, every day there will a vegitarian options in the food menu.
